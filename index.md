1. Power off your mobile device.
2. Hold the volume down and home buttons to enter Download Mode.
3. Connect the device to your computer via a high-quality USB cable.
4. Open the Samsung tab in the software.
5. Click the Read Device button to detect your model.

7. Click Start to begin the flash process.
8. Keep the device plugged in until the indicator says Success.

If you face problems, check these items:
